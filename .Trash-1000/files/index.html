Welcome to SBPGC Website

Shaikh Burhanuddin Post Graduate College (SBPGC), established in 1965, is a renowned private institution in Old Dhaka, Bangladesh. Named after Imaamut Tareeqat Allama Shaykh Sayyid Muhammad Burhanuddin Uwaysi (Rad.), it was founded by the non-political Islamic organization Neda-e-Islam to promote education within an Islamic spiritual framework.

Initially located at 50 Narinda, Dhaka, the college moved to its current campus at 62 Nazimuddin Road in 1968, previously home to the Old Radio Broadcasting Center. SBPGC is affiliated with the National University and offers diverse programs across multiple faculties.

Professional BBA, MBA, and other professional courses

Vision and Mission

SBPGC is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering academic excellence within an Islamic spiritual environment. The college nurtures competent graduates who contribute meaningfully to society.
